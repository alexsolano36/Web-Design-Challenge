Humidity

From this chart comparing City Latitude vs Humidity, there is not a clear relationship between the two variables. This may be because most of the cities in the data set have a humidity percentage of over 60. Though the data does seem to show a scattered and random relationship, we can see that as distance from the equator increases, humidity tends to increase and cluster around 80 to 100 percent.
